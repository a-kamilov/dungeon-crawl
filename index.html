ABOUT THE GAME

Deep within a forgotten realm lies an ancient fortress, abandoned for centuries but still guarding its secrets. As an intrepid explorer, you've discovered the entrance to this mysterious structure and now must navigate its treacherous paths to uncover the truth hidden within its walls.

What happened to the fortress's inhabitants? What treasure awaits in the Reading Chamber? And what ancient power does the Stone Sentinel protect?
